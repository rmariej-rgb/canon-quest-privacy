Common questions

Will I lose my progress if I delete the app?
Yes. All reading progress, notes, and ratings are stored only on your device, with no account or backup. If you're switching phones, back up your device with iCloud or iTunes before you migrate, the same as any other app's local data.

Is my reading data private?
Yes. Nothing you track in the app is ever sent anywhere. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Why isn't a book I expected on the list?
The 100 books are a curated selection, not an exhaustive one. Suggestions for future additions are always welcome at the email above.

The app is behaving unexpectedly. What should I include in a bug report?
Your iPhone model, iOS version, and what you were doing right before the issue happened. Screenshots help a lot.
